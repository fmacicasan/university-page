Research

My thesis goal is to extract patterns and knowledge from customer-generated data in order to drive improvements in the way customers perceive their experience. In this context, the fields of interest are:

  • Recommendation Systems
  • Sentiment Analysis
  • User Profiling with Community Detection

I am part of the Knowledge Engineering Research Group.

My current focus is on overcoming domain dependence in sentiment analysis.
